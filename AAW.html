Autism Awareness Week

Autism Awareness Week runs from the 27th March to the 2nd April. Syngenta UK asked me to create a brand and posters to promote the event and increase Autism awareness within their campus.

Brand intention

The brand aimed to convey the individuality of Autism and the difference between different people with Autism. For that reason, I went with a bold rainbow palette to really highlight inclusion and diversity.

Branding and logos

Starting with the infinity symbol, the project developed through several logos before settling with the final rainbow infinity text. We worked through several iterations surrounding the rainbow and infinity theme.

Poster design

A set of five posters to describe the effects of autism to those who have the condition. Each poster encapsulates the feeling of each effect.
